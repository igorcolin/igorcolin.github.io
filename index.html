I am a machine learning researcher in Huawei's Paris research team. Previously, I was a postdoctoral researcher at INRIA, SIERRA under the supervision of Alexandre d'Aspremont. From 2013 to 2016, I was a Ph.D. student, co-advised by Stephan Clémençon and Joseph Salmon. In 2013, I graduated the Master MVA (machine learning and computer vision) at ENS Cachan and obtained the engineering degree of École des Ponts ParisTech.
